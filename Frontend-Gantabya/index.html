What makes Gantabya different

We treat a trek like a system, not a brochure.

Most agencies sell you a PDF itinerary and a promise. Gantabya is built on four working parts that make the trip more legible — to you, your guide, and the porters carrying your gear.

01 / ROUTE INTELLIGENCE

Altitude & Route Engine

Every route is modeled day-by-day with real elevation gain, so acclimatization isn't a guess — it's data, with rest days built in automatically where they're needed.

02 / SAFETY LAYER

Live Trail Conditions

Trail status, Lukla flight delays, and regional weather are updated directly by our guides — visible on your itinerary before and during your trek.

03 / PEOPLE FIRST

Porter Welfare Ledger

Pay rate, insurance status, and load limits for every guide and porter on your trek, logged and visible — aligned with IPPG standards, not just claimed in writing.

04 / YOUR ROUTE, YOUR PACE

Dynamic Itinerary Builder

Build your trek from modular day-stages — swap, extend, or add a side trip, and your costs, permits, and altitude plan recalculate instantly.

USP 04 — In practice

An itinerary built from real day-stages, not a paragraph.

Each leg of a trek is its own modular block — distance, altitude gain, and a rest-day flag where acclimatization calls for one. Add a side trip and everything downstream recalculates.

This is the structural core that the route intelligence, trail conditions, and permit checklist all plug into.

USP 01 & 03 — Our Promise

Transparency you can check, not just trust.

  • 01

    Verified permits, every trek

    TIMS, conservation area, and national park permits are checked against your route before departure.

  • 02

    18kg porter load limit

    Enforced and logged per trek, in line with International Porter Protection Group guidance.

  • 03

    Insurance on record

    Every guide and porter carries verified insurance — visible in the welfare ledger, not just stated.

  • 04

    Fair, on-time pay

    Wage records are part of the same system that tracks your booking — not a separate informal arrangement.
